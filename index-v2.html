Questions.

How do I know if I need a rebrand?

A rebrand doesn't just attract more work, it attracts better work, and that's the kind of difference that high-end companies need.

If you're lacking clarity about what your brand is, feel like your identity looks outdated, or spend too much time recreating proposals and marketing assets to try and get them right, these are good signs you're ready for a rebrand. Rebranding brings clarity, consistency, and confidence to your business that makes everything you do easier and more effective.

How long does a branding project take?

Most branding and website projects take twelve weeks. We prioritise depth and clarity over rushing the work, but have developed a streamlined process that delivers results as efficiently as possible without compromising on quality.

What's the investment?

We provide transparent proposals outlining project inclusions along with the timeline and investment. Book a call and I'll provide all the relevant details to your business.

I'm hesitant about changing my logo, what should I do?

We've worked with businesses ranging from new startups to companies with over 40 years history. Regardless of the stage of growth, we always make considered, thoughtful changes with business strategy, clients and recognition in mind.

In some cases, we preserve distinctly relevant aspects of a brand that people have come to know, but update them in a way that offers a more refined look. This way you can continue building brand equity as well as reaping the benefits of an identity refresh.
